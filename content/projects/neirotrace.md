### Diagnostic Barrier vs Seamless Trace

An interactive system that visualizes the brain's response to specific stimuli in real-time — a wearable neuro-monitoring device for evidence-based rehabilitation. A transition from clinical discomfort to a wearable, dry-sensor interface that monitors the brain's 'functional trace' in natural environments.
